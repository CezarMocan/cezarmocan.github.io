Distributed Web of Care

2019

Taeyoon Choi

web

In 2019, Taeyoon Choi organized a lecture-performance at the Whitney Museum of American Art, as part of his ongoing Distributed Web of Care project. I was invited to create a mobile-based web experience for the participants, using the garden as a metaphor for the web, in collaboration with Yehwan Song and Jonathan Dahan.

We landed on a design that incorporated representations of nature from Taeyoon's paintings, as well as a series of creatures, the inhabitants of the our networked garden, which walked from one participant to another.

Developed using React and web sockets.
